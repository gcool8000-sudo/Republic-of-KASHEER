Voting Eligibility

  • Voting age: 18+
  • Every eligible citizen has the right to vote freely and safely.

National Assembly Elections

  • Term length: 5 years
  • Legislature: Bicameral (Upper House + Lower House)
  • Goal: Choose representatives who create laws and oversee government.

Selecting the Prime Minister (Parliamentary System)

Citizens elect the National Assembly. Then, the National Assembly chooses the Prime Minister, who forms a Cabinet and leads the government.

  1. Election held for National Assembly seats.
  2. Members form a majority (or coalition).
  3. The majority supports a Prime Minister candidate.

President Selection

The President is elected by National Assembly members and serves mainly as a ceremonial head of state, representing unity and constitutional continuity.

Digital Governance (Special Feature)

Kasheer uses digital governance to improve transparency and services. For example:

  • Secure online services for documents and public requests
  • Open-data portals for budgets and projects
  • Future goal: carefully tested online voting systems

Online voting must be secure, private, and audited to prevent fraud.
