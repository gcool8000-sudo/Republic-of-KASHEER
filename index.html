Welcome

The Republic of Kasheer is a Parliamentary Republic that prioritizes strong institutions, citizen rights, environmental protection, and digital governance.

Core Values

  • Democracy and rule of law
  • Human rights and equality
  • Education and innovation
  • Environmental protection
  • Peaceful diplomacy

Quick Facts

  • System: Parliamentary Republic
  • Legislature: National Assembly of Kasheer (Bicameral)
  • Chambers: Upper House & Lower House
  • Terms: National Assembly (5 years), Prime Minister (5 years)
  • Voting Age: 18+
  • Highest Court: Supreme Court

How to use this website

  1. Visit Government to see the branches and how power is balanced.
  2. Visit Elections to understand voting and leadership selection.
  3. Visit Rights & Policies to see citizen rights, economy plans, and foreign policy.
  4. Visit Team to meet the project members and guide.
